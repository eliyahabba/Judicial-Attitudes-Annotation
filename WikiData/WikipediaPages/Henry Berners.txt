

Henry Denny Berners (18 September  1769, London - 21 September 1852, Woolverstone) was Archdeacon of Suffolk from  1819[1] to 1846.[2]

Berners was educated at St Mary Hall, Oxford.[3]  He was Rector of Erwarton from 1801 to 1835.
