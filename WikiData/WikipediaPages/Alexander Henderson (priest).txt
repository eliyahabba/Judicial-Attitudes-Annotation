
Alexander Henderson (1807 - 1888) was a Scottish Episcopalian priest: he was the Dean of  Glasgow and Galloway from 1859[1] until 1877.

Henderson was educated at King's College, Aberdeen and ordained in 1842. After a curacy in Leith he spent the rest of his career in Hamilton.[2]
