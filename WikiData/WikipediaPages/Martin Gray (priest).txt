
Martin Clifford Gray (born 19 January 1944) was Archdeacon of Lynn from 1999 to  2009.[1]

Gray was educated at West Ham College of Technology and Westcott House, Cambridge. After an earlier career as a process engineer he was ordained deacon in 1980, and priest in 1981.[2] After a curacy in Kings Lynn he held incumbencies in Sheringham and Lowestoft.[3]
